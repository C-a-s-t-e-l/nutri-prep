Frequently Asked Questions

It's simple! Just browse our menu, click on a meal to view details, and add it to your cart. When you're ready, open your cart, proceed to checkout, provide delivery details, and download your receipt. Send that receipt to our Facebook Page to finalize your order and payment.

We currently have a limited delivery zone to ensure freshness. We deliver to most areas in Valenzuela City and North Caloocan. If you live in a nearby area, please send us a message on our Facebook Page to check if your location is serviceable.

We primarily accept payments through Gcash. Once you send us your order receipt on Facebook, we will reply with our GCash details and confirm your total amount, including the delivery fee.

Our meals are prepared fresh without preservatives. For best quality and taste, we recommend consuming them within 3-4 days of delivery. Please keep the meals refrigerated at all times.

You can add special instructions like "no onions" or "extra spicy" in the notes section when adding a meal to your cart. For major changes or allergy concerns, please contact us directly on Facebook before placing your order so we can best accommodate you.
